About Stratrix Technology

We are a team of passionate technologists dedicated to transforming businesses through innovation.

Our Story

Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between technology and business, Stratrix Technology has grown from a small startup to a trusted technology partner for over 100 businesses worldwide.

We specialize in POS systems, CMS solutions, AI automation, CRM & ERP systems, and digital marketing. Our mission is to empower businesses of all sizes with cutting-edge technology solutions that drive growth and efficiency.

Based in Philadelphia, PA, USA, our team of skilled developers, designers, and strategists work together to deliver exceptional results for our clients.
